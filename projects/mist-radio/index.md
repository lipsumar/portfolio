Mist Radio is a browser-based generative music instrument. Record audio, chop it into samples (manually or with automatic detection), then layer those samples in a sequencer where each layer plays its sample on a randomized-but-fixed rhythmic pattern. Layers stack, patterns lock in, and the result is always a little surprising. You're in control, but never fully.
